Complete Staff Guidelines & Policies

×

1. General Conduct & Behavior

• Always maintain professional behavior in all interactions

• Use appropriate language at all times - no profanity or inappropriate content

• Treat all players with respect regardless of age, skill level, or background

• Be patient and helpful when assisting players with questions or issues

• Lead by example - demonstrate the behavior you expect from players

2. Response & Communication

• Respond to player issues and questions promptly (within 30 minutes when online)

• Use clear, helpful language when explaining rules or providing assistance

• Escalate complex issues to senior staff or administration when needed

• Keep player conversations professional and server-related

• Use Discord for staff coordination and important announcements

3. Rule Enforcement

• Follow server rules and policies consistently and fairly

• Apply the warning system appropriately: verbal → written → temporary ban

• Document all disciplinary actions in the staff channel

• Consult with senior staff for permanent bans or complex situations

• Never abuse staff powers or show favoritism to specific players

4. Server Management

• Monitor chat regularly for inappropriate behavior or spam

• Check for griefing and unauthorized modifications to builds

• Assist with server events and community activities

• Help maintain server performance by monitoring resource usage

• Report technical issues or bugs to the administration team

5. Confidentiality & Privacy

• Keep staff discussions and decisions confidential

• Protect player privacy - never share personal information

• Don't discuss disciplinary actions publicly with other players

• Report security concerns or suspicious activities immediately

6. Staff Responsibilities

• Be active and engaged with the community

• Participate in staff meetings and training sessions

• Stay updated on server rules and policy changes

• Provide feedback and suggestions for server improvements

• Maintain your staff account security - use strong passwords

7. Emergency Procedures

• In case of server attacks or major issues, contact administration immediately

• For serious rule violations, temporary ban first then document

• If unsure about a decision, always ask senior staff for guidance

• Emergency contact: support@fozmu.studio

8. Staff Code of Ethics

• Act with integrity and honesty in all situations

• Never use staff privileges for personal gain

• Respect the decisions of senior staff and administration

• Maintain a positive attitude and contribute to team morale

• Report any staff misconduct or policy violations

Last Updated: January 2025
