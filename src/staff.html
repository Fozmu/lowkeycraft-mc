Staff Policy Documentation

01

👋 Introduction & Welcome

Welcome to Fozmu Studios! We are glad to have you aboard and wish you the best in your moderation career with us.

🎯 Our Mission

To create and maintain a safe, enjoyable, and professional gaming environment for all players across our platforms.

📋 Your Role

This documentation lists every duty and responsibility expected of you as a valued member of our moderation team.

02

👥 Staff Positions & Hierarchy

Every position in Fozmu Studios has specific duties and authority levels. Understanding the hierarchy ensures proper communication and workflow.

👑 Lead Developer
Lead Developer of Fozmu Studios. Simply said, this is Fozmu - the Owner of Fozmu Studios with ultimate authority over all operations.
💻 Developer
Developer of Fozmu Studios who helps in development of games and projects. Responsible for bringing new features to games owned by Fozmu Studios.
🔧 Manager
Manager of the Discord Server responsible for bots, moderators, and everything in the server. Also responsible for training moderators and can issue strikes to moderators who violate policies.
🛡️ Lead Moderator
In charge of moderators and speaks for all moderators in Fozmu Studios. Must monitor moderator behavior and inform managers of any violations within the moderation team.
⭐ Senior Moderator
More trusted moderator who has been with Fozmu Studios for a long time. Has more Discord powers and additional in-game commands.
💬 Discord Moderator
Must monitor our Discord server and punish users who violate server rules. Responsible for banning users who violate Discord Terms of Service.
🎮 Game Moderator
Must maintain a safe environment in our games. Responsible for punishing users who violate game rules and banning or kicking exploiters (cheaters).
🌟 Community Manager
Speaks for the community and works to make our community more active. Allowed to host events and game nights. NOTE: This role is not part of our promotion track.
📝 Trial Moderator
Moderator in training who can be removed from the team if they violate any rules during their trial period.
03

📋 General Requirements & Standards

Every member of Fozmu Studios must adhere to these fundamental requirements to maintain our professional standards.

🤝 Professional Standards

  • Must remain professional at all times
  • Must remain respectful to all community members
  • You must remain apolitical during duties
  • You must treat everyone equally

🔒 Security Requirements

  • Do not share your account with anyone
  • 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) is required
  • Report any security breaches immediately
  • Maintain confidentiality of staff information

⚠️ Consequences

You will be demoted if your account has been compromised due to a serious security breach.

You will no longer be part of our staff team if you have been banned from Roblox.

📝 Documentation

ALL KICKS AND BANS MUST BE LOGGED in our #mod-logs channel!

Not logging serious punishments like kicks or bans can lead to demotion or retirement from Fozmu Studios.

04

⚠️ Critical Warning

If no evidence is provided with a ban report, the ban will be revoked!

05

⚡ Striking System & Disciplinary Actions

Our progressive disciplinary system ensures fair treatment while maintaining high standards for all staff members.

⚠️
Strike 1
Serious Warning
First violation of staff policy or rules. Trial Moderators are demoted immediately.
🔥
Strike 2
Final Warning
Can lead to demotion or retirement depending on severity. Acts as final warning for minor issues.
💥
Strike 3
Immediate Retirement
Immediate retirement with blacklist from future applications. Possible server ban for serious violations.

📜 Important Notice

We are not obligated to show evidence of demotions or retirements and can ignore requests to explain the reason for retirement.
